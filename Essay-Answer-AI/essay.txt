

1. Labor and Delivery: This is the process of childbirth, where the baby is pushed through the birth canal and is born.

2. Placental Delivery: After the baby is born, the placenta is expelled from the uterus.

3. Postpartum Care: Postpartum care is the care of the mother after delivery and includes care for the baby, including breastfeeding and other postpartum health care.

4. Infant Development: Infant development is the study of the physical, cognitive, social, and emotional development of an infant from birth to two years of age.
